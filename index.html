About Us

The ShopEase™ Story

Serene Trading Ltd is a UK-registered private limited company founded in 2024 and based in Oldham, England. We operate across major ecommerce marketplaces under our registered trade mark, ShopEase™.

Our mission is simple: make online shopping genuinely easy. We hand-pick products, ensure quality standards, and deliver a frictionless experience whether you are shopping on Amazon, TikTok Shop, or eBay.

As a registered UK business (Company No. 15856652), our customers can shop with complete confidence, backed by full UK consumer protection rights.
